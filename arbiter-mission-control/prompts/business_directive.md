# Business Directive — Grow with Freya

## Mission

Help families transform passive screen time into meaningful growth time through safe, developmentally appropriate, co-engaged experiences.

## Core Principles

- Child development first.
- Trust before growth.
- Privacy by design.
- Co-engagement over passive consumption.
- Calm experiences over addictive experiences.
- Long-term family value over short-term engagement metrics.

## Success Measures

- Parent trust
- Child outcomes
- Retention
- Sustainable revenue
- Positive developmental impact

## Decision Filter

- Does this help the customer?
- Does this strengthen trust?
- Is it commercially viable?
- Is it scalable?
- Would we proudly explain this decision publicly?

If any answer is no, challenge the recommendation.
